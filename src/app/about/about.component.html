About LinkTracker360

At LinkTracker360, we are dedicated to simplifying and enhancing the link tracking experience for professionals in the digital advertising field as well as developers. Our application serves as a trusted companion to those working in media buying, ad serving, and web development.

The inspiration behind LinkTracker360 arose from our own experiences within the digital advertising industry and the challenges we encountered when it came to tracking and verifying links. We understood the importance of accurate link tracking for campaign optimization, source verification, and audience targeting. We also recognized the need to provide developers with a reliable tool to ensure the proper functionality of their website links.

Driven by these insights, we set out to develop an application that would revolutionize link tracking for both digital advertising professionals and developers. Through meticulous research, extensive testing, and invaluable feedback from industry experts, LinkTracker360 was born.

LinkTracker360 serves as your comprehensive link tracking solution, meticulously designed to empower professionals in the digital advertising landscape and developers alike. With its powerful features, you can effortlessly track links, check redirections, and gain insights into the final landing pages behind them. We have also integrated the ability to read click tags from Campaign Manager 360 (CM360) for seamless integration with your ad campaigns.

For digital advertising professionals, LinkTracker360 provides a streamlined and user-friendly tool to optimize marketing campaigns, verify sources, and significantly increase efficiency and productivity. With the ability to track multiple links simultaneously, you can save valuable time and effort, allowing you to focus on other critical aspects of your campaigns. LinkTracker360 empowers you to effortlessly check redirections, gain insights into final landing pages, and ensure the accuracy of your link tracking.

For developers, LinkTracker360 becomes an indispensable asset to ensure the functionality and accuracy of website links. Easily check redirections, diagnose any issues, and maintain the integrity of your web applications.

Experience the convenience, accuracy, and efficiency LinkTracker360 brings to your link tracking endeavors. We are committed to continuous improvement and will continue to refine and enhance LinkTracker360 to meet your evolving needs.
