Instructions to use the Multiple Links Tracker tool

This tool tracks multiple URLs and shows the list of final landing pages only. It can be used by manually adding the initial URLs or by uploading a pre-designed template.

Manually adding the initial URLs:

  • Write or copy/paste your initial URL in the URL text box and then press “Enter” or click on the “Add URL” button. Repeat this step for each of your URLs.
  • After adding the URLs, click on the “Get Landing Pages” button. This may take some time depending on the number of URLs and your internet connection speed.
  • The results will show in a table that includes the initial URLs and the final URLs after applying all redirections.
  • You can export the results to an Excel file by clicking on the “Export Results” button.

Uploading a pre-defined template:

  • Click on the “Download Template” button to download the template.
  • Do not modify the first row of the sheet and do not rename the sheet!
  • Add your initial URLs in column A starting from row 2. It’s preferred not to leave empty rows.
  • Save your template and re-upload it after clicking on the “Upload Bulk” button.
  • After uploading the template, the initials URLs will be shown and you still have the option to remove any one of them.
  • When done, click on the “Get Landing Pages” button. This may take some time depending on the number of URLs and your internet connection speed.
  • The results will show in a table that includes the initial URLs and the final URLs after applying all redirections.
  • You can export the results to an Excel file by clicking on the “Export Results” button.
